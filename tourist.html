10,000 Roses

The 10,000 Roses of Cordova is a place you'd want to visit if you're in Cordova. Not just for its unique beauty, but also as the best spot to unwind. The view of artificial flowers here might make you feel like you're in another place due to its sheer beauty.

Parola Restaurant

The Parola Restaurant, located near the ocean view, offers delicious food, especially seafood. With a relaxing view, it's a great spot for celebrations and various activities.
